Mehmed the Conqueror's Universal Sundial

Made in Istanbul in 1477, this universal sundial was designed for Ottoman Sultan Mehmed II and is the only known original scientific instrument to survive bearing his name.

The instrument consists of a rectangular brass plate with a gnomon set into the central socket, built on a universal formula for determining time from the sun's altitude.
