Ceremonial Helmet & Shield of Mehmed the Conqueror

This ceremonial helmet and shield, representing Sultan Mehmed II, reflect the imperial power identified with the conquest of Istanbul — entirely hand-crafted in brass.

The piece is adorned with verses from Surah Al-Fath, calligraphy reflecting the military conventions of the era, and the Ninety-Nine Names of God.
