Sultan Bayezid II's Astrolabe II

This astrolabe was produced in the 15th century in the Ottoman Empire and dedicated to Sultan Bayezid II. It is adorned with gold and silver inlay techniques.

Displayed at the Museum of Islamic Art in Cairo, this rare piece forms an important part of Ottoman scientific history.

Period9. Hicri / 15. Yüzyıl
Place of ManufactureOttoman Empire
MaterialCopper Alloy with Gold and Silver Inlay
