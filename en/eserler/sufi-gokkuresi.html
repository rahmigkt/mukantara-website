Al-Sufi's Celestial Globe

Displayed at the Museo Galileo in Florence, this piece was originally made by Egnazio Danti but is also popularly known as the "Galileo Astrolabe."

Galileo is known to have used this instrument in his astronomical observations and mathematical calculations.

PeriodRenaissance, 16th century
Place of ManufactureFlorence, Italy
Original MakerEgnazio Danti
MaterialMetal
Reference CollectionMuseo Galileo, Florence
