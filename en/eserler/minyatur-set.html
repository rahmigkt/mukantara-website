Ottoman-Era Miniature Shield, Helmet & Horse Chanfron Set

This special miniature set brings the elegance of Ottoman ceremonial parades and the finesse of Turkish metalwork into a display-case format.

The helmet, shield, and horse chanfron in the set are crafted with traditional engraving techniques and adorned with stones reflecting the ceremonial splendor of the era.
